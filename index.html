About

I am a neurosurgeon with advanced training in the diagnosis and surgical management of brain and spine disorders. My practice emphasizes precision, safety, ethical decision-making, and clear communication with patients and families.

Expertise & Surgical Practice

  • Brain and skull base surgeries
  • Spine surgery for back pain, disc disease, and instability
  • Minimally invasive and endoscopic spine procedures
  • Neuro-trauma and emergency neurosurgery
  • Congenital and pediatric neurosurgical conditions

Vision & Patient Care Philosophy

I believe informed patients make better decisions. My approach combines evidence-based neurosurgery with patient education, realistic counseling, and compassionate care. My goal is to deliver neurosurgical care that is transparent, ethical, and tailored to each patient’s condition and expectations.
